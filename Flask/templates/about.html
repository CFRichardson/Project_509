ADS-509 Project

This project is a part of the ADS-509 Applied Text Mining course in the Applied Data Science Program at the University of San Diego.

Contributers:

  • Gil, Oscar (ogil@sandiego.edu)
  • Richardson, Christopher (cristopherr@sandiego.edu)

Objective

We aim to determine whether negative comments are more helpful in determining the value of a course versus positive comments. Additionally, we will assess if the sentiment of a course's corpus reflects the average score of the course.
